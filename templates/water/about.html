WHO WE ARE

Improving Lives Through Clean Water

D & L Water Solutions Uganda Limited is a Ugandan-based company specialising in UV water purification technology. We were established with a clear mission: to make safe, clean drinking water accessible to every Ugandan.

We understand that access to purified water is not a luxury — it is a basic right. Our UV purification systems combine powerful filtration, UV sterilisation, and reliable stainless steel storage in one complete package.

Locally based team with deep knowledge of Uganda's water challenges
✓
UV purification proven to eliminate 99.9% of harmful pathogens
✓
